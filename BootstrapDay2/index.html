Technology refers to the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes, especially in industry. It encompasses a wide range of tools, machines, systems, and processes designed to solve problems and enhance human capabilities. From communication and transportation to healthcare and education, technology plays a pivotal role in shaping modern life.

Over the years, technological advancements have transformed the way we live and work. Innovations such as artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and the Internet of Things (IoT) have enabled faster processing, smarter decision-making, and greater connectivity across the globe. These developments continue to drive efficiency and open new possibilities across all sectors

Despite its many benefits, technology also presents challenges such as data privacy concerns, ethical dilemmas, and the need for digital literacy. As we move forward, it’s essential to approach technological growth with a balanced perspective—embracing innovation while ensuring it remains inclusive, sustainable, and responsible.
